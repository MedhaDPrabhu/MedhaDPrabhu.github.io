My Travel Blog

This blog describes my experiences at some of the tourist places I have visited over the years.

1. Munnar

< Munnar is known for its lush green tea plantations and pleasant climate.The journey through the winding roads itself was a memorable experience. Walking through the tea gardens and breathing in the fresh mountain air was very refreshing. The calm surroundings and cool weather made the trip relaxing and enjoyable.

2. Sikkim

Sikkim is a beautiful and peaceful state in the northeastern part of India, known for its mountains, monasteries, and natural beauty. During my trip, I visited Gangtok, Tsomgo Lake, and Rumtek Monastery. Tsomgo Lake was especially memorable, as it was surrounded by snow-covered mountains and offered breathtaking views. Visiting the monasteries helped me experience the calm and spiritual atmosphere of the place.
